Kapil Dev:The Man Who Gave India 1st World Cup

India's victorious campaign in the 1983 World Cup in England wouldn't have been possible had Kapil not rescued his side from the jaws of defeat in their fifth match of the 60-over showpiece event.
Batting first India were struggling at 17 for 5 when Kapil came in to bat and smashed a 138-ball 175 while batting with the tail-enders to take the score to 266 for 8 in 60 overs.
Zimbabwe in reply, were bowled out for 235 with Madan Lal (3/42), Roger Binny (2/45) and Kapil (1/32) starring with the ball.
Gavaskar recalled that it was a match that India had to win to enter the semi-final.
