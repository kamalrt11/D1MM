Sandeep Singh:Flicker Singh

Sandeep Singh made his hockey debut in international match when he was just 17 year old in Sultan Azlan Shah Cup in the year 2004. Later on in the same year he became the part of the Asia Cup winning team. The 2 years of the initial years of his career, he achieved everything a teenager can desire. But on 21st August 2006, things become different for him when he hit by an accidental gunshot when he was travelling by Shatabadi express to go and join the Indian national team.
The team was leaving for the World Cup in Africa in next two days. This gunshot left him paralyzed waist down for one year. This was the most crucial year of his life but he doesn’t let it overshadow him. He took it as a challenge and got recovery from his paralysis in just one year and started to stand back on his feet. Not only this, he also played hockey led his country to several victories.
