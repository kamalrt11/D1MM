HIMA DAS:The Golden Girl

By the age of 12 -14 She doesn’t have proper shoes to wear. But not having the shoes doesn’t mean she stop dreaming the medals. If someone asks you what is dedication, just give the example of Hima Das . She is pure example of dedication and passion to achieve the dream. From running on rice fields without shoes to run on the track of Gold Coast, Australia with sponsorship from Adidas Inc., Hima came a long way.
There were the days when Hima wear the locally manufactured shoes and now she is represented world’s top shoe brand company Adidas. From wearing simple low cost shoes to work with world’s top shoe brand Adidas, Hima came a long way.

I am not running after gold medals; I am running after time. And once I get that, gold medals will run after me.


Hima Das
